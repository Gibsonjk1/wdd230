Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

  • Williams Sonoma
  • https://www.williams-sonoma.com/

Williams Sonoma does a good job with visual hierarchy. It seems that their most important thing is to show beauty. I think that's why they focus so much on beautiful images. They typography helps to guide the eye to important things also.

Rule of Thirds

  • Staak Studio
  • https://www.staak.co.uk/

Staak Studio has a main header with images from past work. if you mentally break the image into thirds horizontally and vertically, you can see that their words fall on the line of thirds, the images land within the thirds, etc.

Contrast

  • Remix
  • https://remix.run/

The Remix website does a good job with contrast. The contrast in colors, from pink to black, and bright colored words is eye catching. Also, the typography is contrasting with big bold words introducing principles, and cool smooth lettering for paragraphs.
